Congee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup ~210g white rice*
  • a lot of water
  • few handfulls of sliced dried shiitake mushrooms or 4 shiitake mushrooms
  • a lot of salt
  • 1 chicken breast cut into strips
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tsp Shaoxing rice wine
  • 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  • sesame oil
  • dash ground white pepper or regular I don't care
  • two dashes salt
  • dash sugar
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 2 stalks green onion chopped optional
  • 4 boiled eggs optional
  • 4 tbsp toasted peanuts optional
  1. The night before. Rinse the rice stick in a bag and freeze. Soak the shiitake mushrooms in water.
  2. Morning of boil the water in a medium size pot. Add rice, salt, mushroom and the mushroom water.
  3. When it starts boiling again turn down heat. Cover and simmer for 10mins
  4. Mix the chicken with light soy sauce, oyster sauce, Shaoxing rice wine, cornstarch, sesame oil, white pepper, salt, sugar
  5. After the 10mins are up, stir in the chicken and ginger. Mix for 1min then cover for another minute.
  6. Turn off stove. Stir in green onions. If you're freezing it don't add green onions yet add after you thaw it out.
  7. Dish out with half an egg and peanuts and serve.

Notes

Most short and medium varieties work just not Basmati.
