Grandma K's chocolate chip cookies

This recipe makes quite a lot of cookies.

Depending on how many people you're making this for I recommend cutting it in half or even thirds.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups shortening
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 5 eggs
  • 5 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips or add-in of choice

Directions

  1. Cream together 2 cups shortening, 2 cups brown sugar, 1 cups white sugar
  2. Add 2 tsp baking soda, 2 tsp vanilla, 1/2 tsp salt, 5 eggs, 5 1/2 cups flour, and 1 1/2 cups chocolate chips or add-in of choice and mix
  3. Scoop or roll into balls
  4. Bake at 350f or 176 c for a base of 15mins then till golden brown
