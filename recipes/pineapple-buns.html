Pineapple Buns

Ingredients

  • heavy cream
  • milk
  • eggs
  • milk powder
  • sugar
  • a lot of flour*
  • yeast
  • tsp salt
  • baking soda
  • baking powder
  • shortening
  • vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a mixing bowl, add 2/3 heavy cream, 1 cup milk, and 1 egg. Let sit for ~15 mins or until room temp. Then add 1/3 cup sugar 5 cups flour*, 1 tbsp yeast, and 1 1/2 tsp salt. Mix for 15mins if you're using an electric mixer or 10mins if you're doing it by hand
  2. Cover the bowl with a damp cheese cloth or saran wrap and let set for 1 hour or till the dough grow 1 1/2 times its orginal size*.
  3. Lightly flour your working surface. Dump dough out and knead dough till smooth again. Cut into 12 pieces. Form into balls and place on a parchment lined baking sheet, or dont. Let rise for 1 hour.
  4. During that hour, make the topping. Grind up 2/3 cup sugar until fine. Put the sugar and 1/4 cup milk powder in a bowl mix and make sure there are no lumps.
  5. Add 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1/2 tsp baking soda, and 1/4 tsp baking powder and mix. Add 1/4 cup shortening, 2 tablespoons milk, 1 egg yolk, and 1/8 teaspoon vanilla extract and mix.
  6. ~10mins before the hour is up, roll out topping and cut into 12 circle.
  7. Preheat the oven to 350f or 175c. Top the dough with the topping and brush with an egg yolk. You can skip it up the buns won't turn golden.
  8. Cook for a base of 10min then till tops are golden brown or bottoms are brown.

Notes

  • *A mix of bread flour and cake flour is best but all-purpose works fine.
  • If it's too cold to proof properly, boil some water in a bowl put it in the bottom rack of the oven and place the bowl with the dough directly above it. You may need to change the water a few times during the course of in hour.
