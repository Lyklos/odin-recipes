Korean Ground Beef

You needn't follow this recipe to a T. This is just a template feel free to add or take away as you please and make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • veggie or some sort of cooking oil
  • 2 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1 inch ginger minced
  • 2/3 cup soy sauce
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sesame oil
  • couple dashs of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 2/3 cup green onion
  • toasted sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Cook rice per directions.
  2. In a medium size pan, heat oil over a medium flame. Once heated, add 2 cloves garlic and 1 inch ginger. Cook 1 min or until fragrant.
  3. Add 1lb ground beef and chop thoroughly.Cook until a light brown. Drain excess fat with a spoon or papertowel.
  4. Combine 2/3 cup soy sauce, 4 tbsp sugar, 1 1/2 tbsp sesame oil, and red pepper flakes in a bowl. Stir until sugar is dissolved.
  5. Add sauce to the pan lower heat and simmer till cooked.
  6. Mix in 2/3 cup green onion and cook for 1 min.
  7. Serve over steamed rice and sesame seeds.
