Cumin Beef

Here's the recipe for cumin beef I learned at the Chinese restarant I work at. It serves 2.

Ingredients

  • ~1lb beef
  • half Bell pepper
  • half a Onion
  • Green onions
  • ginger
  • Cumin powder
  • Cumin seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cooking oil
  • rice

Directions

  1. Cook rice per instructions. Cut 1lb beef into 1 inch slices. Cut half a bell pepper and half an onion into ~1 inch slices. Cut ~1/2 tbsp ginger and ~1/2 green onion into small slices.
  2. Blanch veggies and set aside. Fry beef for around 4mins or until brown, set aside.
  3. Lightly coat pan with oil and start heat on high. Add ginger and green onion to pan and cook for 30 secs.
  4. Add beef and veggies then stir.
  5. Add all the seasonings till it tastes good.
  6. Serve with rice.
